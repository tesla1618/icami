## General

### What is ICAMI 2026?

ICAMI 2026 is the International Conference on Advanced Machine Intelligence, held at Multimedia University (MMU), Malaysia in December 2026. The conference is organized around four tracks: trustworthy and autonomous AI, generative and quantum intelligence, healthcare AI, and AI for industry and smart systems.

### Who can attend?

ICAMI 2026 is open to researchers, practitioners, students, and industry professionals worldwide. Attendance is not contingent on having an accepted paper  non-author registrations are available.

### What are the conference dates?

The conference is scheduled for **December 2026**. Exact dates will be confirmed on the [Important Dates](/important-dates) page.

## Submission

### What is the paper submission deadline?

Submissions are due in **July 2026**. Refer to the [Important Dates](/important-dates) page for the confirmed date.

### What topics are covered?

ICAMI 2026 runs four tracks: General, Trustworthy & Autonomous Intelligence; Generative, Quantum & Scalable Intelligence; AI for Healthcare & Intelligent Medical Systems; and AI for Industry, Infrastructure & Smart Systems. Full topic lists are on the [Tracks](/tracks) page.

### What format should my paper follow?

All submissions must conform to the IEEE conference paper format. Templates, formatting guidelines, and page limits are provided on the [Call for Papers](/call-for-papers) page.

### Can I submit work that has been published elsewhere?

No. Submissions must be original work that is neither previously published nor currently under review at another venue. Dual submissions will be desk-rejected.

### How will I be notified of the decision?

Acceptance and rejection notifications will be sent to the corresponding author's registered email address by **October 2026**.

### When is the camera-ready deadline?

Final versions of accepted papers are due in **November 2026**. Authors are expected to address all reviewer feedback prior to submission of the camera-ready copy.

## Registration

### How do I register?

Registration details and the submission portal will be announced on the [Registration](/registration) page. Note that at least one author per accepted paper must complete registration for the paper to be included in the proceedings.

### Are there different registration categories?

Yes. Fee structures  including potential reductions for students and participants from qualifying countries  are listed on the [Registration Fees](/registration/fees) page.

### Can I get a refund if I cancel?

Refund and cancellation terms will be specified in the registration portal. Consult the [Registration Guidelines](/registration/guidelines) for current policy.

## Venue & travel

### Where is the conference held?

ICAMI 2026 takes place at **Multimedia University (MMU), Malaysia**. Venue address, directions, and nearby accommodation options are listed on the [Venue](/venue) page.

### Do I need a visa to attend?

Visa requirements vary by nationality. Malaysia maintains visa-free or visa-on-arrival arrangements with a number of countries; verify your status with your nearest Malaysian embassy or consulate. If the conference will be issuing invitation letters to support visa applications, this will be communicated through the registration portal.

### Will the conference be hybrid (online + in-person)?

ICAMI 2026 is planned as a fully in-person event. If virtual participation options are introduced, they will be announced on the website.

## Publication

### Where will accepted papers be published?

Accepted and presented papers are planned for submission to the IEEE Xplore Digital Library, subject to IEEE's quality standards and editorial policies.

### Will there be a best paper award?

Yes. Details on award categories and selection criteria will be posted on the [Awards](/awards) page.
