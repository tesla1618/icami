The ICAMI 2026 registration policy has been updated.

## Final baseline

- Base registration fee: **USD 500**

## Discount policy

- Research4Life Category A: **80% off**
- Research4Life Category B: **50% off**
- IEEE member: **25% off**

## Important rule

Discounts are **not cumulative**. If multiple discounts apply, the system uses the **highest single discount**.
