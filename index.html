About the conference

ICAMI brings together researchers and practitioners advancing machine intelligence that works in the field: rigorous methods, careful evaluation, and measurable impact. We emphasize selective, high-quality programs and papers that move ideas into practice.

The program spans foundations, systems, perception, language, trust, edge deployment, and high-impact applications—with a consistent focus on applied research and responsible innovation.

Featured tracks

All tracks→

  • Foundations of Applied Machine Intelligence

    Learning theory, optimization, generalization, and theoretical grounding for deployed intelligent systems.

  • Representation Learning and Generative Models

    Self-supervised learning, generative modeling, multimodal and foundation models across domains.

  • Intelligent Perception Systems

    Vision, multimodal perception, 3D understanding, video, and imaging for science and industry.

  • Language Intelligence and Reasoning Systems

    LLMs, retrieval and knowledge integration, reasoning, planning, and multilingual NLP.

  • AI Systems and Scalable Intelligence

    Distributed training, efficient inference, MLOps, and systems for large-scale AI workloads.

  • Trustworthy, Secure, and Responsible AI

    Explainability, fairness, robustness, privacy, and governance for high-stakes deployment.
